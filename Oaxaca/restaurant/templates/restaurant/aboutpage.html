Welcome to Oaxaca!

Founded in 4/20/1969, Oaxaca was originally a coffee shop ran by the Crips, that took the west coast by storm with its signature weed, meth and uranium.

However in 1971 the one and only Snoop Dogg was born, legends say that he hotboxed his mothers womb as he came out smoking 5 joints. After overseeing Oaxaca for 1 day he decided it was time he took over and so the much loved Oaxaca became the restaurant we know and love today.

After the prophets' arrival he added many loved items onto the menu such as the Snoop Hot Dogg and the unforgettable Garlic Bread.

Over the years Oaxaca menu became bigger and bigger with dishes such as the award winning Sigma Meat Balls and spectacular Kidney Beans.

However in 2000 Oaxaca took a massive L in customers as the chef of Oaxaca, Gilbert Jeronimo the 3rd, spontaneously combusted due to a methane gas leak. That is when the gods sent a message to Eminem and told him to become a partner to Oaxaca.

And so Eminem changed the restaurants menu and brought in world renowned dishes such as Moms Spaghetti and Methane Gas along side Oaxaca new head chef Madame Gertrude Brunhilda.

Ever since Eminem joined the business Oaxaca has been thriving even partnering up with more people of power such as Donald J Trump to bring classics such as the EPIC Covfefe.

For all the Karens among us we also offer vegan options and options for all allergies.

Here at Oaxaca, we hope to make your experience one of a kind with our spectacular food, atmosphere and history.
